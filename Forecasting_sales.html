Forecasting Sales Using Power BI

Project Objective

This project aims to develop a predictive Power BI dashboard capable of forecasting sales and analyzing key business dimensions. By integrating DAX-based measures and interactive visualizations, the model provides data-driven insights into future sales trends, customer behavior, product performance, and profitability to support strategic decision-making.

1. Sales Forecast

A time-series forecast was developed using a dynamic Date Table and DAX measures to project sales for the upcoming 12 months. Interactive visuals compare actual and forecasted sales, highlighting peak sales periods and potential growth opportunities.

2. Customer Analysis

Customer segmentation and retention metrics were created to understand purchasing behavior. The dashboard provides demographic insights by age, gender, and location, alongside predictive measures for customer acquisition and retention.

3. Product Analysis:

This section identifies top-performing and underperforming products through forecasted sales projections. Using DAX filters and visual ranking, it highlights future high-demand categories and potential low-performing products for optimization.

4. Profitability Analysis

Forecasted cost and profit measures were developed to predict gross margins and overall profitability trends. Interactive line and bar charts display forecasted profit growth and support financial performance monitoring.
